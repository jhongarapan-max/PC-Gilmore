    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions

    What are your store hours?

    We are open Monday to Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary.

    Do you offer warranty on products?

    Yes! All products come with manufacturer warranty. We also provide a 30-day service warranty on all repair work performed by our technicians.

    How long does a custom PC build take?

    Most custom PC builds are completed within 2-3 business days, depending on parts availability. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

    Do you offer on-site repair services?

    Currently, we only offer in-store repair services. You can bring your device to our store at Gilmore IT Center for diagnosis and repair.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept cash, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), GCash, Maya, and bank transfers. Installment options are available for select purchases.

    Can I get a quote before committing?

    Absolutely! We provide free consultations and quotes for all services. Just fill out the contact form above or give us a call.
